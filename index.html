About Us

Building Excellence Since 2010

At Domino Construction, we bring together specialists from every trade under one roof. From small home repairs to large commercial projects, we handle it all with precision and professionalism.

We work with individual clients, corporations, industrial facilities, and serve as a trusted subcontractor on major construction projects throughout Toronto and the Greater Toronto Area.

Our commitment to quality, safety, and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred choice for renovation and restoration projects across the region.
